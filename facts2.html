Economics

The economy of Japan is very similar to the one in
American, being a free based open market. The country
has long been an Economic Giant in the global marketplace.

The current GDP of Japan is growing at a much faster rate than
it has been for many years. On the other hand, the exchange rate
for the Japanese Yen to the US Dollar, the lowest it's been since the 1990s.
