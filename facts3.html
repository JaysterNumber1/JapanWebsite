Culture

The culture of Japan is a very religous and traditional
culture, where both are celebrated often. It is a
very long-standing culture, but not afraid of adapting
and evolving with the modern times and technology.

Japanese Matsuri (or Festivals) bring out a lot of energy
inside people. These times of celebration are done with
quiet a bit of noise and pizzazz. The streets will be lined
with a wide variety of vendors with both food and knick-knacks.
