Geography

The island of Japan is just off the coast of mainland
Asia. It is between the Sea of Japan to the west and
the Pacific Ocean on the east. The land of Japan is
made up of a variety of terrain, but is mainly rugged
and mountainous.

The climate of Japan is quiet varied, much like the US.
The climate is much more tropical and warm in the more
southern prefectures and much colder and snowy in the
more northern prefectures.
